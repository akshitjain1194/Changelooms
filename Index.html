Changeloom's evolution over the years

Since 2005-06, the Changeloomers journey has been woven under shifting names — Changelooms, LLJ, CL-LLJ, Changemakers — as the programme responded to new partners, new geographies and new ideas of youth leadership. This is that thread, followed end to end.
